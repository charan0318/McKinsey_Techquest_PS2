## Risk-Adjusted Success Criteria

### Quality Guardrails (Must Not Degrade)
- 30-day readmission rate ≤ baseline (6.1%)
- Avoidable ED rate ≤ baseline (75.6%) - *wait, this should improve*
- Member satisfaction ≥ baseline
- MA Star ratings ≥ current
- Provider satisfaction ≥ baseline
- Access metrics (PCP availability, network adequacy) ≥ baseline

### Financial Guardrails
- Total program cost (tech, staffing, vendor) < 50% of realized savings
- No single intervention >20% of total program budget without steering committee approval
- Actuarial assumptions documented and validated quarterly

### Operational Guardrails
- Data freshness SLAs met >95% of time
- KPI dashboard uptime >99.5%
- Care management caseload ratios within industry standards (1:150 high-touch)
- Member complaint rate <2% of engaged population

## Definition of Done (Program Level)

The program is considered successful at Month 18 if:

1. ✅ **Financial**: Cumulative realized savings ≥ $1M (validated by actuarial)
2. ✅ **Clinical**: All quality guardrails met, ≥3 clinical KPIs at stretch targets
3. ✅ **Member**: Disenrollment rate <8%, cost/access disenrollment <40%, transparency adoption >25%
4. ✅ **Operational**: All Phase 1-3 gates passed, platform sustainable, team capable
5. ✅ **Strategic**: VBC contracts active, predictive models in production, SDoH integrated
6. ✅ **Governance**: Steering committee functioning, data governance mature, documentation complete

## Continuous Improvement Loop

```
Measure → Analyze → Intervene → Monitor → Recalibrate → Measure
   ↑                                                           ↓
   └────────────────── Monthly/Quarterly/Annual ───────────────┘
```

**Recalibration Triggers**:
- KPI off-target for 2 consecutive months
- Savings realization <50% of model for 2 consecutive quarters
- Quality guardrail breached
- Major regulatory change (CMS, state, federal)
- Significant membership shift (>10% change)
