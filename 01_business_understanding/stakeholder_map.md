## Governance Structure

### Steering Committee (Monthly)
**Chair**: CMO & CFO (co-chairs)  
**Members**: All VPs + Analytics Director  
**Purpose**: Strategic decisions, budget approval, escalation resolution, go/no-go on major interventions

### Working Groups (Weekly/Bi-weekly)
| Working Group | Lead | Members | Focus |
|---------------|------|---------|-------|
| High-Cost & Specialty | VP Medical Mgmt + VP Pharmacy | CM, Pharmacy, Analytics | Intervention design, PA criteria, CM protocols |
| ED Diversion & Avoidable Utilization | VP Medical Mgmt | CM, Network, Member Experience, Analytics | Triage protocols, UC network, member comms |
| Member Retention & Experience | VP Member Experience | Digital, Marketing, Analytics, Actuarial | Transparency tools, churn prediction, outreach |
| Network & Value-Based Care | VP Network Mgmt | Contracting, Quality, Analytics, Actuarial | Adequacy, VBC contracts, provider scorecards |
| Data & Analytics Governance | Director, Analytics | Data Engineer, BI, Data Stewards | DQ, architecture, access, prioritization |

### Data Stewardship Council (Monthly)
**Chair**: Director, Healthcare Analytics  
**Members**: Claims Ops Mgr, Membership Dir, Network Mgr, Analytics Consultant  
**Purpose**: Data quality review, definition alignment, access requests, metadata management

| Exploratory Analysis | R | A | C | C | C | C | I | I |

| Intervention Design | C | I | R | R | R | R | C | A |
| Actuarial Deliverables | C | I | C | C | I | I | R | A |
| Member Communications | I | I | C | I | C | R | I | A |
| Provider Communications | I | I | C | C | R | I | I | A |
| Implementation Planning | C | C | R | R | R | R | C | A |

**R** = Responsible (does the work)  
**A** = Accountable (final approval)  
**C** = Consulted (provides input)  
**I** = Informed (kept updated)

## Escalation Path
1. **Working Group Lead** → Resolves within working group
2. **Analytics Director** → Cross-working group dependencies, resource conflicts
3. **Steering Committee** → Strategic decisions, budget, policy changes
4. **CMO/CFO** → Final authority on clinical/financial trade-offs
