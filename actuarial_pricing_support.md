ANALYTICS -> ACTUARIAL/PRICING INTEGRATION FRAMEWORK
====================================================

1. EXPERIENCE STUDIES & TREND ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------
* PMPM trends by LOB, region, product, metal tier (monthly rolling 12)
* Cost driver decomposition: Unit cost vs Utilization vs Mix shift
* High-cost claimant impact on trend (reinsurance layer analysis)
* Specialty pharmacy trend separate from medical trend
* Avoidable utilization trend (ED, readmissions) as leading indicator

2. RATE SETTING & PRODUCT PRICING
---------------------------------
* Credibility-weighted PMPM by rating area / product
* Risk score calibration: Actual vs Predicted by PCP quality tier
* Network adequacy pricing: OON leakage cost built into premium
* Benefit design modeling: Cost-sharing impact on utilization (ED copay, Rx tiers)
* Medicare Advantage bid pricing: Star rating impact (readmissions, BH follow-up)

3. RESERVING & IBNR
-------------------
* Lag triangles by service category (IP, ED, Specialty Rx, Professional)
* High-cost claimant development patterns (longer tail)
* Avoidable ED shift impact on completion factors
* Behavioral health utilization as predictor of future medical cost

4. RISK ADJUSTMENT & STAR RATINGS (MA)
--------------------------------------
* HCC coding completeness gaps by provider quality tier
* Readmission reduction -> Star rating improvement -> Bonus revenue
* BH follow-up rates -> Star rating -> Quality bonus
* Medication adherence (Specialty Rx) -> Star rating

5. NETWORK CONTRACTING & VALUE-BASED CARE
-----------------------------------------
* Provider-level TCO (Total Cost of Care) by attributed panel
* Quality-cost efficiency frontier: Identify high-quality/low-cost PCPs
* Episode-based costing: Maternity, Joint replacement, Oncology
* Shared savings calculation: Benchmark vs Actual by ACO/PCP group
* Site-of-care differentials: ASC vs HOPD, Home infusion vs Hospital

6. SCENARIO PLANNING & STRESS TESTING
-------------------------------------
* Medical cost trend scenarios: Base / High / Low (CMS, internal)
* High-cost claimant shock scenarios (gene therapy, CAR-T)
* Regulatory impact: No Surprises Act, IRA drug pricing, Medicaid redetermination
* Membership churn impact on risk pool morbidity
